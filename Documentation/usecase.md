ID	Use Case	Actors	Use Case Description
UC001	Manage Projects	Lecturer	Lecturer creates, manages, submits, assigns, and picks projects based on subject syllabus, including using AI to generate milestones.
UC002	Create Projects	Lecturer	Lecturer creates new projects with description, objectives, and milestones based on syllabus.
UC003	Manage Own Projects	Lecturer	Lecturer views and edits their own projects (pending, approved, denied).
UC004	Submit Pending Projects	Lecturer	Lecturer submits created projects to Head Department for approval.
UC005	Assign Projects to Classes	Lecturer, Head Department	Lecturer or Head Department assigns approved projects to classes.
UC006	Pick Projects for Teams	Lecturer	Lecturer selects projects from approved list for specific teams.
UC007	Manage Classes	Lecturer	Lecturer manages assigned classes, including viewing details and resources.
UC008	View Assigned Classes	Lecturer	Lecturer views list and details of their assigned classes, including students, projects, and teams.
UC009	Manage Resources for Classes	Lecturer	Lecturer uploads and manages resources (files, docs, slides) for classes.
UC010	Manage Teams & Workspaces	Lecturer, Student	Users manage teams, workspaces, progress, and contributions.
UC011	Create/Manage Teams	Lecturer	Lecturer creates and manages teams in assigned classes, including information and members.
UC012	Manage Team Members	Lecturer	Lecturer adds, removes, or edits members of teams.
UC013	Monitor Team Progress	Lecturer, Student	Users monitor overall team progress and completion percentage.
UC014	Monitor Member Contributions	Lecturer, Student	Users monitor individual member's contribution percentages.
UC015	Manage Team Milestones	Lecturer	Lecturer manages milestones based on project objectives.
UC016	Create/Manage Milestones Questions	Lecturer	Lecturer creates and manages questions for milestones to guide team development.
UC017	View Team Workspaces	Lecturer	Lecturer views the workspace of teams, including cards, tasks, and subtasks.
UC018	Manage Checkpoints	Lecturer, Student	Users create, assign, submit, and mark done checkpoints within milestones.
UC019	Manage Communication	Lecturer, Student	Users handle real-time communication including chat, meetings, and collaborative tools.
UC020	Chat with Teams	Lecturer, Student	Users communicate via persistent real-time chat.
UC021	Call/Schedule Meetings	Lecturer, Student	Users initiate video/audio meetings or schedule future ones with notifications.
UC022	Use Real-time Tools	Lecturer, Student	Users utilize interactive whiteboard, text editor, and other synchronized tools for collaboration.
UC023	Evaluation & Feedback	Lecturer, Student	Users provide and view evaluations and feedback for teams, members, milestones, and checkpoints.
UC024	Evaluate Teams/Members	Lecturer	Lecturer evaluates and gives feedback to teams and individual members at project end.
UC025	Evaluate Milestones Answers	Lecturer, Student	Users evaluate and feedback on members' answers to milestones questions.
UC026	Evaluate Checkpoints Submissions	Lecturer	Lecturer evaluates and feedbacks on team checkpoint submissions.
UC027	View Peer Evaluations	Lecturer	Lecturer views peer evaluations to inform future judgments.
UC028	Manage Resources	Lecturer, Student	Users manage resources (files, docs, slides) for classes, teams, milestones, and checkpoints.
UC029	Manage Files/Docs for Classes/Teams	Lecturer, Student	Users upload, edit, and manage files and documents for classes or teams.
UC030	Use AI Assistance	Lecturer, Student	Users interact with AI chatbot for brainstorming, guidance, suggestions, and analysis of progress/contributions.
UC031	Notification System	Lecturer, Student	System sends real-time and email notifications for events like milestone completions, submissions, uploads, messages, and feedback.
UC032	View Classes & Teams	Student	Student views details of assigned classes and teams.
UC033	View Assigned Classes/Teams	Student	Student views list and details of their assigned classes and teams.
UC034	View Subjects/Syllabus	Student	Student views details of subjects and syllabi.
UC035	View Milestones/Checkpoints	Student	Student views details of team milestones and checkpoints.
UC036	Mark Done Milestones/Checkpoints	Student	Student (team leader) marks milestones or checkpoints as done.
UC037	Create/Manage Checkpoints	Student	Student (team leader) creates and manages checkpoints, assigns members.
UC038	Submit Checkpoints	Student	Student submits entries for team checkpoints.
UC039	Create/Manage Cards/Tasks/Subtasks	Student	Student creates and manages cards, tasks, and subtasks in the team workspace.
UC040	Answer Milestones Questions	Student	Student answers questions associated with milestones.
UC041	Evaluate Other Members	Student	Student provides feedback for other team members at project end.
UC042	View Evaluations/Feedback	Student	Student views evaluations and feedback from lecturer or peers.
